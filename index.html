How the Invention Works

  • ESP32 collects weather and vibration data from sensors.
  • Data is sent via SIM800L to Google Apps Script (stored in Google Sheets).
  • Website fetches JSON from Google Sheets and updates in real-time.
  • Fishing tips and alerts are generated based on temperature, wind, rain, pressure, sea level, and humidity.
  • Vibration alerts trigger SMS and call automatically if thresholds are exceeded.
